About Me

Intuition,
engineered.

I am Donna Mae Lat, a second-year BS Information Technology student at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) Sto. Tomas, bridging the gap between user needs and technical execution as an aspiring UI/UX Designer. I specialize in crafting intuitive, human-centered interfaces and prototyping engaging digital experiences where clean aesthetics meet seamless functionality.

My work is driven by a deep fascination with how human-centered design and engineering discipline can come together to solve real-world problems. Ultimately, my goal is to architect seamless digital experiences that leave a lasting impact as I grow in the tech industry.

When I'm not writing code or refining visual identities, you can probably find me exploring new UI trends or fueling my creativity with a great playlist and endless curiosity.
